WHY CHOOSE COURSEPATH?

Mentors can create, update, and manage courses with detailed descriptions, learning materials, and structured content to provide students with an organized learning experience.

Students can browse available courses, enroll with a single click, and access all learning resources from their personal dashboard anytime.

Enrolled students can follow a structured learning path, complete lessons in sequence, and build their knowledge step by step through organized course content.

Mentors can monitor enrolled students, track their learning progress, and evaluate course completion to provide better guidance and support.

The platform provides separate dashboards for mentors and students, ensuring that each user can access only the features and information relevant to their role.

Designed with a clean and intuitive interface, CoursePath makes it easy for mentors to manage courses and for students to learn without unnecessary complexity.
